Welcome to THE ONE

Official information page for the com.duckerforge.theone Android app.

THE ONE is a non-custodial Solana dApp distributed via the Solana dApp Store. Players spend SOL on tickets, chests, and badges. Spent SOL is split between an on-chain prize pool and a fixed operator fee. At a publicly announced "Draw Day", the prize pool is distributed by an automated, server-side function.

Prize distribution

  • 50% to a single ticket holder, picked at random with probability proportional to tickets held.
  • 30% split equally between the top 5 wallets by points.
  • 20% split equally between wallets ranked #6 to #10 by points.
